About School

Our school stands as a proud institution where tradition meets transformation. Since its founding in 1971, the school has been committed to nurturing young minds with a strong foundation in Islamic values, academic excellence, and personal discipline. From the early Montessori years to the rigorous ICSE curriculum, every stage of learning is designed to foster intellectual growth, moral integrity, and social responsibility. The school’s warm, inclusive environment encourages curiosity, creativity, and character—preparing students not just for exams, but for life. As a beacon of holistic education in Bhatkal, New Shams School continues to inspire generations, shaping tomorrow’s leaders with faith, knowledge, and purpose.

Our Teachers

At New Shams School, our teachers are the heart of our institution. Each educator brings a wealth of experience, deep subject knowledge, and a passion for nurturing young minds. From Montessori to ICSE levels, they guide students with patience, creativity, and a strong sense of responsibility. Our faculty not only uphold academic excellence but also instill Islamic values, discipline, and confidence in every child. With continuous training and a commitment to holistic development, our teachers ensure that learning is meaningful, inspiring, and rooted in care.

Vehicles Facility

Reliable Transportation Across Bhatkal and Beyond
New Shams School offers a safe and efficient transport service with a fleet of 17 well-maintained school buses. Our vehicles cover all major areas in Bhatkal and nearby localities, ensuring that students from every corner can reach school comfortably and on time. Each bus is operated by trained drivers and support staff, prioritizing safety, punctuality, and care. Whether from town centers or quieter neighborhoods, our transport network connects families to quality education with ease and reliability.

Our Students Feedback

At New Shams School, we believe that listening to our students is key to continuous growth. Through regular feedback sessions, students share their learning experiences, classroom environment, and suggestions for improvement. Their honest reflections help us refine our teaching methods, enhance facilities, and create a more engaging and supportive atmosphere. Whether it’s appreciation for a caring teacher or ideas to improve school life, every voice matters. We value student feedback as a vital part of building a school that truly serves its learners.
